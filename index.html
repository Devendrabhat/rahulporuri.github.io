I am Rahul, currently pursuing a BS & MS degree in Physics with a minor in Chemistry at IIT Madras. I'm passionate about astronomy & astrophysics, applied computation and electronics in the context of astronomy. I have had some prior experience in the field, ranging from participation in winter schools and workshops to working on summer projects as an intern. For a broad overview of my research experience, you can visit the Research page.

I am currently working with Dr. L. Sriramkumar on the generation and evolution of tensor perturbations during various inflationary scanarios and in bouncing universes. I developed a python code to numerically evaluate the evolution of tensor perturbations during inflation and to estimate the tensor power spectrum at super-Hubble scales. You can find the relevant code and results in this Github repository. A project report pertaining to this work can be found here. I am also developing a code to numerically evaluate the tensor bi-spectrum.

I try to maintain an active github account and a blog, the former of which I use for course work and side projects. One of my side projects includes making fun physics simulations on an ipython notebook using ipywidgets. You can check them out on the Blog page. I also make academic learning projects involving astronomical data, available here.
