I'm Rahul and I graduated with a Dual Degree (B.S. & M.S.) in Physics from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai, India. As part of my curriculum, I worked on two thesis projects with Dr. L. Sriramkumar of the Department of Physics, IIT Madras, titled 'Generation of gravitational waves during inflation' and 'Numerical evaluation of the inflationary tensor bi-spectrum using Python'. In short, the theory of inflation solves a few of the drawbacks of the hot big bang model of the universe and I numerically evaluated two specific theoretical predictions of various models of inflation. The reports are available here(pdf) and here(pdf) and the relevant Python codes for the two projects are available on a Github repository here.

I started using Python during a summer project at the end of my fourth year but I've been using it extensively ever since, for course projects, for my thesis projects, for pet projects and so on. Most of my work is available on my Github profile.
