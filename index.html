I'm Rahul, currently pursuing a BS & MS degree in Physics with a minor in Chemistry at IIT Madras. I'm passionate about astronomy & astrophysics, applied computation and electronics in the context of astronomy. I have had some prior experience in the field, ranging from participation in winter schools and workshops to working on summer projects as an intern. For a broad overview of my research experience, you can visit the Research page.

I try to maintain active github and blogger accounts, the former I use to store my course work and side projects and the latter I use as a daily journal of interesting things that I come across. One of my side projects includes making fun physics simulations on an ipython notebook using ipywidgets. You can check them out on the Blog page. I also make projects involving astronomical data, data which upon visualization can help understand the underlying concept. They are available here.

Any comments you have regarding my work or my projects are appreciated and always welcome.
