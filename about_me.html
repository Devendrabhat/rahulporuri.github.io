Hi, I'm Poruri Sai Rahul, an undergraduate at the Indian Institute of Technology, Madras.I'm currently pursuing a BS & MS degree in Physics.My interests lie in astronomy, astrophysics and instrumentation.

I did my schooling in Ongole, Andhra Pradesh and my +2 at Narayana Junior College, Hyderabad. After being coached/trained to write IIT-JEE for 2 years, I finally made it through with a rank of 2944. I joined IIT Madras to pursue a degree in BS & MS in Physics.
