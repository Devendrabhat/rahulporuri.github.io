EPISODE III
The Search for Grad School

After being battle hardened in Episode I - The JEE Ordeal and surviving IIT in Episode II - Conquering Courses, our antagonist now sets out in search of a grad school. Fairly sure of his interests that now lie in the field of Astronomy & Astrophysics, he is now looking for universities with stimulating research.

Having experienced research under the guidance of Prof. Anand Narayanan at IIST, Trivandrum, India and Prof. Jeremy Mould, Center for AStrophysics & Supercomputing, Swinburne University of Technology, Melbourne, Australia, he now feels confident in putting a strong fight for a PhD position. His experience at the Pulsar Observatory for Students, ARIES Training School on Observational Astronomy and the IIST Astronomy & Astrophysics schools will also come in handy in his endeavours.

Stay tuned for our antagonist's efforts over the next 6 months in preparing for GRE, in refining his outlook and in search of PhD positions.
