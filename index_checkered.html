My research interests lie in Astronomy & Astrophysics. I've worked with Prof. Jeremy Mould to construct a Tully-Fisher relation for SAMI galaxies and I've worked with Prof. Anand Narayanan to replicate and extend the results of Richards et al. (2001) on the Colors of Quasars to 146,659 quasars in the SDSS DR9.
I've also attended the Pulsar Observatory for Students program at the Ooty Radio Telescope, NCRA-TIFR, the ARIES Training School on Observational Astronomy and the IIST Astronomy & Astrophysics School.
